Singapore No.1 Confinement Nanny Agency

PEM Confinement Nanny Agency was established in 1987 and has been in the industry for 30 years. We strive to provide mummies with the utmost confinement care in Singapore at an affordable price. PEM Confinement Nanny Agency is the largest Confinement Centre in Singapore with over 400 nannies and is the first confinement nanny agency to be licensed by Ministry of Manpower (MOM).

Laundry for Mother & Baby

Nanny will do the laundry, including washing, hanging and folding the clothes. (Only baby’s clothes will be handwash)

Confinement Bath

Nanny will prepare confinement bathing herbs for the mother to wash up.

Pro Breastfeeding

24 Hours Baby Care

Experienced and Trained Nannies

PEM Confinement Nanny Agency is the first agency in Singapore that has an in-house training centre where nannies undergo special classes on breastfeeding, proper hygiene practices and confinement diet planning to ensure that they are competent for future assignments. Nannies are also given a challenging test at the end of their training to examine their level of skills and knowledge acquired during training.

Giving You A Peace of Mind

Annual Blood Test for Nannies

With the newborn safety in mind, all nannies in PEM are required to go through an annual medical checkup for Hepatitis B and HIV to ensure that our nannies are safe to work for our customers.
